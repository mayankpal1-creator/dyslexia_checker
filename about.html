About DyslexiAI

Understanding and Supporting Dyslexia

Our Mission

DyslexiAI is dedicated to early detection and support for individuals with dyslexia. We use modern technology to analyze handwriting and speech patterns, providing accurate assessments and personalized learning recommendations.

What is Dyslexia?

Dyslexia is a specific learning disability that affects the ability to read and spell. It's not related to intelligence and affects people of all ages. Early detection and appropriate intervention can significantly improve outcomes.

Our Approach

  • Advanced handwriting analysis using computer vision
  • Speech pattern recognition for phonetic assessment
  • Personalized learning recommendations
  • Gamified learning experience to maintain engagement
  • Progress tracking and detailed analytics

Why Early Detection Matters

Early identification of dyslexia can lead to timely intervention, preventing frustration and boosting confidence. Research shows that children who receive appropriate support early are more likely to succeed academically.
